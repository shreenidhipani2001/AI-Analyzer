**Q: What is the expense ratio of Mirae Asset Large Cap Fund (Direct Plan)?**

The Direct Plan of Mirae Asset Large Cap Fund has a Total Expense Ratio (TER) of approximately 0.54% per annum (as of the latest factsheet). The Regular Plan TER is higher at approximately 1.57% per annum. Always refer to the latest factsheet for the current TER as it may change monthly.

**Q: What is the lock-in period for Mirae Asset ELSS Tax Saver Fund?**

ELSS (Equity Linked Savings Scheme) funds have a mandatory lock-in period of 3 years from the date of each investment allotment. This means each SIP installment is locked in for 3 years from its individual allotment date, not from the start of the SIP. After the lock-in, units can be redeemed at any time.

Source: https://www.miraeassetmf.co.in/docs/default-source/application-form-kim/16-kim---mirae-asset-elss-tax-saver-fund_rf_29052026.pdf
Last updated from sources: 2026-06-02

**Q: Is there an exit load on Mirae Asset Flexi Cap Fund?**

Mirae Asset Flexi Cap Fund charges an exit load of 1% if units are redeemed within 1 year from the date of allotment. There is no exit load if units are redeemed after 1 year. This applies to both lump sum and SIP investments.

Source: https://www.miraeassetmf.co.in/docs/default-source/application-form-kim/15-kim---mirae-asset-flexi-cap-fund_sept2025.pdf
Last updated from sources: 2026-06-02

**Q: What is the riskometer level of Mirae Asset Large & Midcap Fund?**

Mirae Asset Large & Midcap Fund (formerly Emerging Bluechip Fund) is classified as "Very High" risk on the SEBI riskometer. This classification reflects the fund's exposure to large and mid-cap equities, which are subject to significant market volatility.

Source: https://www.miraeassetmf.co.in/docs/default-source/fachsheet/active-factsheet---may-2026.pdf

**Q: How do I download my Consolidated Account Statement (CAS)?**

You can request a Consolidated Account Statement (CAS) from CAMS by visiting their statement page, entering your PAN and the registered email address, and selecting the statement period. The CAS covers all mutual fund holdings across all RTAs (CAMS and KFintech) and is delivered to your registered email.

Source: https://www.camsonline.com/Investors/Statements/Consolidated-Account-Statement
Last updated from sources: 2026-06-02

**Q: What is the minimum SIP amount for Mirae Asset NYSE FANG+ ETF Fund of Fund?**

The minimum SIP amount for Mirae Asset NYSE FANG+ ETF Fund of Fund is ₹99 per installment. The minimum lump sum investment amount is ₹5,000. Please refer to the latest KIM for the most current minimums.
